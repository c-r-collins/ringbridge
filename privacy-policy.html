RingBridge Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Overview

RingBridge is a single-purpose utility that reads workout data written to Apple Health by Garmin Connect and Strava, and writes active energy, step count, and distance back to Apple Health. All processing happens entirely on your device.

Data We Access

Read from Apple Health: Workout records, active energy burned, step count, and distance samples originating from Garmin Connect and Strava sources.

Written to Apple Health: Active energy burned, step count, walking/running distance, cycling distance, and swimming distance — derived solely from the Garmin/Strava workout data already in your Health library.

Data We Do Not Collect

RingBridge makes zero network requests. No health data, personal information, usage statistics, crash reports, or analytics of any kind are transmitted off your device. We have no servers, no accounts, and no cloud infrastructure.

Data Storage

RingBridge stores a small list of processed workout identifiers in your device's local storage (UserDefaults) to prevent duplicate syncs. This list never leaves your device and contains only opaque UUIDs, not health measurements.

Third-Party Services

RingBridge does not use any third-party SDKs, analytics frameworks, or advertising services.

Children's Privacy

RingBridge does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes

If this policy changes materially, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised date.
